Particle physics data in your classroom?

MINERvA is a experiment at Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory studying the scattering of neutrinos from nuclei. MINERvA physicists in collaboration with high school teachers from Rochester, New York, and Duluth, Minnesota, have developed exercises using MINERvA's data that tie into goals of the standard high school curriculum.
